# Lab: Capturing Credentials through Social Engineering

## Objective
In this lab, the objective was to send a phishing email with a malicious link to a user and set up a malicious website to capture the user’s login credentials. 


## Steps taken
- Ensured Thunderbird is installed and updated on Kali Linux
- Used the social engineering toolkit (SET) to set up a malicious website to capture credentials 
- Configured Thunderbird for Gmail
- Used Thunderbird to create a phishing email with a malicious link to Kali Linux
- Tested the phishing email

## Key takeaways
This lab taught me how simple it is to set up a phishing site and use social engineering to capture a target's credentials. This could either be used in a targeted spear phishing campaign or a mass phishing campaign.

## Relevance
Phishing and Social Engineering have always been one of the most widely used tactics to steal a victim's credentials. They still work regularly now, as they can be very sophisticated, or rely on people not knowing what a phishing email looks like.
